Ask MustangDriver — high-level requirements (mustangdriver.com)
A chatbot, "Ask MustangDriver," that answers any Mustang question, grounded in Mustang Driver's article archive, with clickable links to the source articles it used
The chatbot can fall back to live web search (and recall lookups) for anything the archive doesn't cover — current prices, news, events
As people chat, the app quietly builds a profile: their car (year/trim/generation), mods already installed, mods they want, and their goals — all stored in memory
A "My Garage" page showing their car with arcade-style stats (Power, Handling, Braking, Aero, Style — Need for Speed vibes) and a cool visual of the car
The Garage shows suggested upgrades based on their profile and chat history, with each upgrade showing its estimated stat boost (e.g., "+6 Handling")
Upgrade suggestions are sourced from Mustang Driver's partner vendors first (search restricted to their sites), with product, price, and a link
Suggestions link back to relevant Mustang Driver articles (reviews, install guides) whenever they exist
Works in a browser on both phone and laptop
Everything reinforces the business: engagement (chat), retention (garage/profile), and vendor/advertiser value (partner-first upgrade suggestions)
